### 1.1 MONITOR - Track Brand Visibility Across AI Engines

**What It Does:**
Apex continuously monitors brand mentions across 7+ AI-powered search engines in real-time. Instead of manual querying, the platform automates daily checks across every major AI platform your customers use.

**AI Platforms Covered:**
- ChatGPT (OpenAI's GPT-4/4-Turbo)
- Claude (Anthropic Claude 3 Opus/Sonnet)
- Gemini (Google's Gemini Pro/Ultra)
- Perplexity AI
- Grok (xAI)
- DeepSeek
- Microsoft Copilot

**Key Metrics Tracked:**
1. **Mention Rate** (0-100%)
   - Percentage of tracked prompts mentioning your brand
   - Example: "Out of 50 AI prompts tested, your brand appears in 30" = 60% mention rate

2. **Position Score** (1-10)
   - Where your brand ranks when mentioned
   - Position 1 = First recommendation (highest visibility)
   - Position 5+ = Lower visibility

3. **Share of Voice**
   - Your brand mentions vs. total competitor mentions
   - Shows competitive positioning in the AI ecosystem
   - Example: "You own 35% of category mentions in ChatGPT"

4. **Sentiment Analysis**
   - Positive, neutral, or negative AI context
   - Tracks how AI frames your brand

5. **Platform-Specific Performance**
   - Which AI engines mention you most
   - Which don't mention you (gap identification)

**Value for Different Segments:**
- **E-commerce brands**: Know if ChatGPT recommends your products
- **SaaS companies**: Track if Perplexity cites you for solutions
- **Digital agencies**: Monitor all client brands in one dashboard
- **Global enterprises**: Identify regional AI visibility gaps

**Business Impact:**
Users see:
- Real-time visibility score (0-100)
- Week-over-week trend indicators
- Competitive benchmarking (anonymized)
- AI-specific recommendations

### 1.3 AUDIT - Comprehensive Technical Site Analysis for AI Visibility

**What It Does:**

**Dimension 1: Technical Score (30% weight)**
- **Page Speed** (FCP, LCP, TTI metrics)
- **Mobile Optimization** (responsive design, touch targets)
- **Core Web Vitals** (LCP, FID, CLS)
- **Technical SEO** (sitemap, robots.txt, canonicals, HTTPS)
- **Crawlability** (internal linking, broken links, URL structure)

**Dimension 2: Content Score (35% weight)**
- **Content Quality** (depth, originality, clarity)
- **Keyword Optimization** (target keywords, LSI keywords)
- **Content Structure** (proper headings, paragraph length)
- **Internal Linking** (relevant anchor text, topical clustering)
- **Metadata** (title tags, meta descriptions, alt text)

**Dimension 3: AEO Score (35% weight) - MOST CRITICAL**
- **Structured Data** (schema.org markup quality)
- **Answer Format** (direct answers, FAQ sections, how-to format)
- **Citation Worthiness** (authoritative content, proper sourcing)
- **Semantic Richness** (entity recognition, topic depth)
- **AI Accessibility** (clean HTML, logical flow, fast loading)

**Audit Reports Include:**
1. **Quick Audit** (30 seconds)

   - Top 5 critical recommendations

2. **Comprehensive Audit** (2-10 minutes)
   - Full technical analysis

   - Historical comparison

3. **Continuous Monitoring**
   - Automated ongoing audits
   - Immediate issue alerts
   - Improvement tracking
   - Regression alerts

**Issue Severity Categorization:**

### 1.4 SMART RECOMMENDATIONS ENGINE - THE CORE DIFFERENTIATOR

**What Makes It Different:**
Searchable shows you DATA (scores, mention rates, issues). Apex shows you ACTION (what to do, why, how, expected impact).

#### **The Problem Apex Solves:**
- Competitor tracking: ✓ (everyone does this)
- Audit reports: ✓ (everyone does this)
- But WHO interprets the data? → USER
- But WHAT should they do? → USER has to figure out
- But WILL it work? → NOBODY knows

**The Apex Solution:**
AI automatically analyzes:
1. Your GEO score breakdown
2. AI platform mention patterns
3. Competitor activities
4. Your website content gaps
5. Available market opportunities

Then generates:
- **Priority Score** (0-100) combining:
  - Impact potential (40% weight): How much visibility will improve?
  - Confidence score (30% weight): How sure are we this will work?
  - Implementation ease (20% weight): How much effort required?
  - Urgency (10% weight): How time-sensitive is this?

#### **Five Types of Recommendations:**

**1. Content Gap Recommendations**
- What: Topics/products mentioned by AI but missing from your website
- Why: AI models found this info elsewhere; it's discoverable
- How: Create/expand content on this topic
- Impact: "+15% mention rate in ChatGPT for this topic"
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks

**Example:**
- AI regularly mentions competitors' "product roadmap"
- Your website has no roadmap page
- Recommendation: "Create public product roadmap"
- Expected impact: "Capture 25% of competitor's roadmap searches"

**2. Schema & Structured Data Recommendations**
- What: Missing schema.org markup (FAQPage, Product, Organization)
- Why: AI models extract and cite structured data preferentially
- How: Copy-paste code snippet provided
- Impact: "+20% citation rate for pages with schema"
- Timeline: 1-2 hours

**Example:**
- Audit finds no FAQPage schema on /faq
- Recommendation: "Add FAQPage schema (3 minutes to implement)"
- Code provided: Ready-to-copy JSON-LD
- Expected impact: "ChatGPT will extract FAQ answers directly"

**3. Multi-Language Content Recommendations**
- What: Content opportunities in African languages (Zulu, Xhosa, Swahili)
- Why: 60% of African internet users search in local languages; low competition
- How: Auto-translate existing content or create new localized pages
- Impact: "+40% visibility in [language] AI queries"
- Timeline: 1-2 weeks per language

**Example:**
- Brand serves South African market
- English content ranks well
- Zulu translation: 3,200 monthly searches, no competition
- Recommendation: "Create Zulu version of top 5 product pages"
- Expected impact: "Dominate Zulu AI searches in 30 days"

**4. Voice Search Optimization**
- What: Content restructuring for conversational queries
- Why: 40% of AI queries are conversational; requires Q&A format
- How: Rewrite content with shorter sentences, Q&A format
- Impact: "+30% voice query mentions"
- Timeline: 2-3 hours per page

**Example:**
- Product page: Complex technical specifications
- Recommendation: "Rewrite product page as Q&A format"
- Suggested titles: "What are the technical specs?", "How do I integrate this?"
- Expected impact: "Appear in Perplexity voice search results"

**5. Competitor Gap Recommendations**
- What: Features/content competitors have that you don't
- Why: Low-hanging fruit to close visibility gap
- How: Implement specific feature or content format
- Impact: "+10-20% share of voice vs competitors"
- Timeline: Varies

**Example:**

- Recommendation: "Create comparison content (template provided)"
