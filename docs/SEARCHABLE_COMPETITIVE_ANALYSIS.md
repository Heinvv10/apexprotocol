## 🎯 CORE WORKFLOW & METHODOLOGY

### 1. The Prompt Pyramid Strategy

Searchable teaches users to build prompt libraries using a **4-tier pyramid approach**:

```
    Peak: Adjacent Topics (10-30 prompts)
       ↑ Related topics for authority building
       Example: "What is [related concept]?"

    Top: Problem/Solution (20-50 prompts)
       ↑ Capture users before they know solutions exist
       Example: "How can I [achieve goal]?"

    Middle: Category Queries (10-20 prompts)
       ↑ Compete for category dominance
       Example: "What are the best [category] for [audience]?"

    Foundation: Brand Queries (5-10 prompts)
       ↑ Establish baseline brand awareness
       Example: "What is [Your Brand]?"
```

**Total Recommended**: 45-110 prompts per brand

### 2. Daily Tracking Cycle

**Automated Workflow**:
1. **Query All AI Platforms Daily** - Run all prompts across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity
2. **Extract Mentions** - Identify if brand appears in responses
3. **Calculate Metrics**:
   - **Mention Rate**: How often brand appears (0-100%)
   - **Average Position**: Ranking when mentioned (1-10)
   - **Share of Voice**: Your mentions vs total category mentions
   - **Sentiment**: Positive, neutral, or negative

4. **Generate Reports**:
   - Daily summaries
   - Weekly trend analysis
   - Monthly strategic recommendations

### 3. Optimization Cycle (Monthly)

**Recommended Cadence**:
1. **Run for 2 Weeks** → Collect baseline data
2. **Analyze Patterns** → Identify high/medium/low performers
3. **Create Content** → Target zero-mention prompts
4. **Optimize Existing** → Improve partial mentions
5. **Add Variations** → Expand successful prompts
6. **Repeat Monthly** → Continuous optimization

## 🔢 SCORING SYSTEM BREAKDOWN

### Overall Health Score Calculation

```
Overall Health = (Technical × 0.30) + (Content × 0.35) + (AEO × 0.35)
```

### Technical Score (0-100) - 30% Weight

**Components & Weighting**:
- **Page Speed (30%)**: FCP, LCP, TTI, TBT
- **Mobile Optimization (25%)**: Responsiveness, touch targets, viewport
- **Core Web Vitals (20%)**: LCP, FID, CLS
- **Technical SEO (15%)**: Sitemap, robots.txt, canonical tags, HTTPS, structured data
- **Crawlability (10%)**: Internal linking, broken links, redirects, URL structure

**Score Ranges**:
- 90-100: Excellent - Technical foundation is solid
- 70-89: Good - Generally well-optimized
- 50-69: Fair - Several technical issues
- 30-49: Poor - Significant problems
- 0-29: Critical - Major issues severely impacting site

### Content Score (0-100) - 35% Weight

**Components & Weighting**:
- **Content Quality (30%)**: Depth, original information, clear writing, grammar
- **Keyword Optimization (25%)**: Target keyword usage, keyword density, LSI keywords
- **Content Structure (20%)**: Heading hierarchy, paragraph length, readability
- **Internal Linking (15%)**: Quantity, relevant anchor text, topical clustering
- **Meta Data (10%)**: Title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, alt text

**Score Ranges**:
- 90-100: Excellent - Comprehensive, well-optimized content
- 70-89: Good - Quality content with minor opportunities
- 50-69: Fair - Needs improvement in structure or depth
- 30-49: Poor - Thin or poorly optimized content
- 0-29: Critical - Very thin content, major rewrite needed

### AEO Score (0-100) - 35% Weight

**Components & Weighting**:
- **Structured Data (25%)**: Schema.org markup, rich snippets, knowledge graph
- **Answer Format (25%)**: Clear direct answers, FAQ sections, how-to formats
- **Citation Worthiness (20%)**: Authoritative content, proper sourcing, expert bios
- **Semantic Richness (20%)**: Entity recognition, topic depth, related concepts
- **AI Accessibility (10%)**: Clean HTML, logical flow, fast loading

**Score Ranges**:
- 90-100: Excellent - Highly optimized for AI, likely to be cited
- 70-89: Good - Well-formatted for AI with some opportunities
- 50-69: Fair - Basic AI compatibility, needs structured data
- 30-49: Poor - Difficult for AI to parse, missing key elements
- 0-29: Critical - Not AI-friendly, major restructuring needed

## 📈 KEY METRICS TRACKED

### 1. Visibility Score (0-100)
Measures how prominently your brand appears when AI models mention you.

**Calculation Factors**:
- Mention frequency
- Position in responses
- Context quality
- Sentiment

### 2. Presence Rate (0-100%)
Percentage of tracked prompts that mention your brand at all.

**Example**:
- 50 prompts tracked
- Brand mentioned in 15 responses
- Presence Rate: 30%

### 3. Share of Voice
Your brand mentions as percentage of total category mentions.

**Use Case**: Competitive benchmarking
- Compare against industry leaders
- Track trends over time
- Identify gaps and opportunities

### 4. Platform-Specific Performance

**Different AI platforms have different behaviors**:
- **ChatGPT**: Most conservative with citations, values authoritative sources
- **Claude**: Detailed responses, values comprehensive content
- **Gemini**: Strong Google integration, favors recent content
- **Perplexity**: Always provides cited sources, real-time web search
- **Grok**: Access to real-time data, conversational tone

### Issue Severity Levels

- Security vulnerabilities

**High (Orange)**:
- Significant impact on rankings
- Poor user experience

**Low (Blue)**:
- Nice-to-have optimizations
- Minor improvements

### Issue Prioritization Algorithm

Searchable automatically prioritizes fixes by:
1. **Impact Score**: How much it affects your scores
2. **Effort Level**: Easy, medium, or hard to fix
3. **Page Importance**: Based on traffic and conversions
4. **Issue Frequency**: How many pages are affected

**Quick Wins** (High impact, low effort):
- Missing alt text
- Broken internal links
- Missing meta descriptions
- Duplicate content
- Image optimization
- **Expected improvement**: 5-15 points

**Long-Term Projects** (High impact, high effort):
- Site-wide redesign
- Content rewrite projects
- Technical infrastructure changes
- Structured data implementation
- **Expected improvement**: 20-40 points over 3-6 months
