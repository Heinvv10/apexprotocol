# Searchable.com - Comprehensive Competitive Research Report
*Research Date: December 8, 2025*

---

## 🎯 Executive Summary

**Searchable** is an AI Search Optimization (AEO) platform that helps brands capture visibility across AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Claude, and others. They've positioned themselves as the "operating system for AI search optimization" at a critical inflection point where consumer search behavior is shifting from traditional search engines to AI-powered conversational interfaces.

**Key Metrics:**
- Founded: 2025 (4 months ago)
- Team Size: 5 employees (2-10 range)
- Funding: $4M pre-seed at $40M valuation
- Customer Base: 1000+ marketing teams, 4,500+ signups in 12 days post-launch
- LinkedIn Followers: 11,345
- Location: London, UK

## 📊 Business Model & Value Proposition

### Core Value Proposition
**Main Headline:** "Visibility & Analytics from AI Search — and the actions to drive growth"

**Core Message:**
"Capture millions of clicks from customers discovering new products and brands through ChatGPT, Google Overviews, and more."

### The Problem They Solve
Traditional SEO is becoming less effective as consumers shift to AI-powered search:
- 700M monthly users on ChatGPT
- 450M on Google Gemini
- 30M on Perplexity and Claude
- 40-55% of consumers in top sectors use AI search for purchasing decisions
- Brands invisible to AI engines are losing massive amounts of potential traffic
- AI-generated responses account for 13.1% of U.S. desktop queries in 2025
- Generative AI referral traffic to SMB sites was up 123% in first half of 2025

### Their Solution - 4 Core Product Areas

#### 1. **MONITOR** - AI Visibility Tracking & Analytics
- Track brand mentions across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI, Copilot, DeepSeek, Grok
- Monitor visibility scores, citation frequency, and competitive positioning
- Connect GA4 and Google Search Console for unified analytics
- Interactive AI Agent for data queries in plain English
- Real-time alerts when rankings or citations change

#### 2. **CREATE** - AI-Optimized Content Generation
- Generate content using brand's own data and tone of voice
- AI-powered article creation (from thought-leadership to programmatic blogs)
- Content scheduling and publishing
- SEO and AEO optimization built-in
- Supports multiple content types optimized for AI extraction

#### 3. **AUDIT** - Technical Site Analysis
- Comprehensive site audits to identify AI visibility issues
- Schema markup validation
- Structured data optimization
- Content architecture analysis for AI extraction
- Identifies gaps affecting AI engine understanding

#### 4. **ANALYTICS** - Competitive Intelligence
- Visibility reports across all AI platforms
- Citation tracking and historical trends
- Competitor share-of-voice analysis
- Performance correlation with traditional search metrics

## ⚠️ Competitive Threats & Risks

### Immediate Threats (0-6 months)

1. **Profound's Capital Advantage**
   - $58.5M vs Searchable's $4M (14.6x difference)
   - Can outspend on marketing, engineering, sales
   - Sequoia backing adds credibility and network

2. **Otterly's Price Disruption**
   - $29 entry point vs Searchable's $50
   - Could capture budget-conscious solo creators
   - Forces Searchable to justify premium pricing

3. **New Entrants**
   - Low barriers to entry (wrap API calls around LLMs)
   - $45M+ VC funding attracting more startups
   - Category getting crowded quickly

### Medium-Term Threats (6-18 months)

4. **Legacy SEO Tool Integration**
   - Semrush, Ahrefs adding AEO features
   - Massive existing customer bases (millions of users)
   - Can bundle AEO at no extra cost

5. **Platform Risk**
   - OpenAI, Google, Anthropic could restrict API access
   - AI platforms could build native brand visibility tools
   - Algorithm changes could disrupt value prop

6. **Category Commoditization**
   - AEO could become table-stakes feature
   - Pricing pressure as competition increases
   - Differentiation harder over time

### Long-Term Threats (18+ months)

7. **Platform Consolidation**
   - AI platforms (OpenAI, Google) building brand tools directly
   - CMS platforms (WordPress, Shopify) integrating AEO natively
   - Could eliminate need for third-party tools

8. **Enterprise Players Scaling Down**
   - BrightEdge, Conductor launching SMB tiers
   - Leveraging enterprise credibility for SMB market
   - Could squeeze Searchable from above
