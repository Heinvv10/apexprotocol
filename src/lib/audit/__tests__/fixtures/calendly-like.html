Easy scheduling ahead

Calendly is your hub for scheduling meetings professionally and efficiently, eliminating the hassle of back-and-forth emails so you can get back to the work that matters.

Why teams choose Calendly

Calendly connects to your calendar and figures out your real availability in seconds. Invitees pick a time that works. You both get a confirmation, calendar invite, and reminder — no email ping-pong, no double bookings.

Whether you're a single rep running demos or a 500-person sales team coordinating across timezones, the workflow stays the same: share a link, let people book, and meet.

How does Calendly work with Google Calendar?

Calendly reads your Google Calendar to compute free/busy time, respects buffers you define, and writes new events back when someone books. You can connect multiple calendars and control which ones are used for availability versus conflict-detection.

Can I brand the booking page?

Yes — Team and Enterprise plans support custom logos, colors, and domains so the booking experience stays on-brand end to end.
