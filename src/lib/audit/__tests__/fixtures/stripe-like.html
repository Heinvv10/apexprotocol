Payments infrastructure for the internet

Millions of companies of all sizes use Stripe online and in person to accept payments, send payouts, automate financial processes, and ultimately grow revenue across the internet.

Why Stripe

Stripe was built for developers who need a reliable, battle-tested payments stack. Our APIs are clean, our SDKs are polished, and our documentation is the best in the industry. You get real-time webhook events, instant payouts, and a global platform supporting over 135 currencies.

We power payments for Shopify, Amazon, Google, and many more of the world's most ambitious companies. The scale of our infrastructure means your checkout flow benefits from continual improvements in reliability, fraud detection, and authorization rates.

What can I build on Stripe?

Accept payments on your website, build marketplaces with programmatic payouts, issue physical or virtual cards, manage subscriptions with flexible billing, and reconcile everything through a single unified dashboard.

Each Stripe product is designed to work standalone or integrated with the others, so you can start small and expand as your business grows without re-platforming.

How do developers get started?

Start by signing up for a free test-mode account. You get production-grade APIs, sandboxes, and keys immediately — no credit card required. From there you can integrate in minutes using our official SDKs for every major language.

Most teams go from first test payment to live checkout within a day. Our quickstart guides cover the happy path and our API reference documents every edge case you will hit along the way.

Is Stripe reliable?

Our global infrastructure has been engineered for 99.999% API uptime. We run active-active across multiple regions and auto-failover in seconds. Real-time status and historic incident reports are publicly available.
